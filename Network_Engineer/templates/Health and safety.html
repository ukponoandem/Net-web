The Safety of Our Team is Our Top Priority

At Net.com, health and safety are at the core of everything we do. Whether working on-site or remotely, we prioritize safety at all times, adhering to the highest industry standards. We maintain a rigorous training program to ensure that 100% of our team is fully trained and understands the importance of safe working practices in every situation. All of our engineers and staff are trained to the highest qualifications and standards, including compliance with relevant certifications and industry standards. We proudly adhere to CSCS, CPCS, NVQ, and ISO 45001 certifications, reflecting our commitment to safety and industry excellence. We equip our employees with top-tier Personal Protective Equipment (PPE), ensuring they are not only safe but also presentable on site. Our commitment to this high standard has earned us a reputation for having some of the most professional and well-equipped employees in the network engineering field. Our safety track record speaks for itself, with over X million hours of labor completed without a single reportable accident. This achievement demonstrates our dedication to maintaining a safe work environment and reinforces our reputation for reliability and professionalism. We also leverage cutting-edge technology, including QR code tracking, to allow both our team and clients to quickly access a summary of each employee’s training, competencies, and upcoming certifications. This ensures transparency and real-time awareness of safety standards at all times.

The Health, Safety and Wellbeing Of Our Employees Remain a Top Priority, and We Continually Strive To Ensure a Safe and Supportive Environment For Everyone Involved in Our Projects.
